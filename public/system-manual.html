MUBS M&E System

System Description & User Manual

1. System Overview

The MUBS Monitoring & Evaluation (MUBS M&E) System, also referred to as the Strategic Performance System, is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to track, manage, and evaluate strategic activities and operational tasks across the institution.

The system streamlines workflows by aligning daily staff tasks with overarching institutional strategic goals, providing real-time tracking, structured reporting, and performance evaluation mechanisms.

2. General Usage Guide

To use the system effectively, follow these general steps:

  1. Login: Access the system via the https://mubsme.mubs.ac.ug . Enter your credentials. The system will automatically direct you to the appropriate dashboard based on your assigned role.
  2. Navigation: Use the sidebar or top navigation menu to access different sections relevant to your role.
  3. Dashboards: Upon logging in, your dashboard provides a high-level summary of pending items, active tasks, and performance metrics. Always check your dashboard first for urgent alerts.

3. Admin Module

Roles: System Administrator, Strategy Manager

The Admin Module is the control center of the MUBS M&E system. It allows high-level management of users, strategic standards, and institution-wide tracking.

Admin Dashboard

The Dashboard provides a holistic view of the system's usage and overall institutional performance metrics. Key UI elements include:

  • Stat Cards (Top Row): Provide high-level numbers for Total Activities, Overall Progress (%), System Users, and Delayed Activities.
  • Quick Actions Card: Contains immediate buttons for frequent administrative tasks like Create New User, Add Strategic Activity, and reviewing delayed items.
  • Critical HR Alerts Card: Displays a list of staff members requiring urgent attention, such as contract expirations or pending leaves. Colored badges indicate the alert type.
  • Office/Faculty Performance Card: Shows horizontal progress bars for each department, visually indicating completion rates (Green for on-track, yellow for caution, red for delayed).
  • Recent System Activity Card: A timeline log showing the latest actions taken within the system.

Users Management Page

Allows administrators to add new users, edit details, and manage system access.

  • Stat Cards: Top numbers showing System Users, Active Accounts, and Defined Roles.
  • Sub-Tabs: Toggle buttons to switch between the "All Users" view and the "Departments & Units" management view.
  • All Users Table: Lists every user with columns for User details (name/date added), Email, Role (colored badges), and Department. It includes a search bar and role filter dropdown. Actions allow editing and deleting users.
  • Departments & Units Table: When selected, shows departments and their parent faculties. Includes actions to create new sub-units or edit existing structures.

Other Admin Pages (Sub-sections)

  • Tracking: Allows administrators to monitor the progress of specific activities across all departments. Provides a bird's-eye view to identify bottlenecks or delayed projects.
  • Strategic Planning: The core configuration area where Strategy Managers define high-level strategic goals, set up standards, and create the templates that all departments must align with.
  • Reports: Generates high-level, institution-wide analytical reports on performance metrics and compliance to present to university leadership.

4. Department Head Module

Roles: Department Head, Unit Head, HOD

Designed for academic and administrative leaders to oversee their specific department's performance, manage their staff, and ensure departmental activities align with the institution's strategic goals.

Department Head Dashboard

A focused overview of the department's current standing. Key UI elements include:

  • Hero Banner: Displays the specific Department/Faculty name.
  • Warning Strip: A dismissible alert at the top indicating if any department staff require HR attention.
  • Quick Action Tiles: Four clickable tiles directing the head to Strategic activities, Department tasks, Submissions & reviews, and System warnings.
  • Strategic Activity Progress Table: Shows activities assigned from the institutional strategic plan, displaying status badges (e.g., On Track, Delayed) and a visual progress bar for each.
  • Department Tasks Table: Similar to the strategic table, but for operational tasks strictly created within the department.
  • Recent Submissions Sidebar: A compact list displaying staff task submissions that have recently come in, along with their status (e.g., Reviewed, Pending) and a button to view/review them.

Other Department Head Pages (Sub-sections)

  • Strategic Activities: Aligns department tasks with broader institutional strategic goals. Allows heads to map their activities directly to the standards set by Admins.
  • Department Tasks & Activities: Facilities for managing operational tasks and general activities specific to the department that may not be directly tied to the strategic plan.
  • Staff Management: Allows heads to view and manage the staff members assigned to their department, including reviewing their workloads and HR alerts.
  • Evaluations & Submissions: Provides tools to review, provide feedback, and evaluate the quality of tasks submitted by staff members.
  • Department Reports: Generates focused reports detailing the department's internal performance, staff productivity, and strategic alignment.

5. Staff Module

Roles: Staff, Viewer

The Staff Module is the operational engine of the system where individual contributors receive task assignments, report progress, and manage their daily workflows.

Staff Dashboard

A personal workspace highlighting the user's active tasks. Key UI elements include:

  • Stat Cards: Quick overview showing Total Tasks assigned, tasks currently In Progress, tasks Overdue, and Completed tasks.
  • Quick Action Tiles: Four central buttons providing instant access to Submit report, View my tasks, Submissions (feedback), and Check deadlines.
  • Today's Focus Areas Card: Displays high-priority assigned tasks that need immediate attention. Each entry shows the task title, a progress bar, status, and an "UPDATE" button that opens a modal to submit work directly from the dashboard.
  • Upcoming Deadlines Sidebar: A vertical list of tasks nearing their due date. It utilizes urgent badges (e.g., "OVERDUE", "URGENT" for <2 days, "SOON") to highlight time-sensitive items.

Other Staff Pages (Sub-sections)

  • My Tasks: A detailed view of all tasks assigned to the staff member, including instructions, requirements, and due dates.
  • Process Steps & Tasks: For complex workflows, staff can view and complete specific individual steps within a larger process.
  • Submit Report & Submissions: The interface used to officially submit completed work, upload necessary evidence or documents, and view a history of past submissions.
  • Feedback: An area to review comments and evaluations provided by the Department Head on previously submitted tasks, facilitating continuous improvement.
  • Deadlines & Notifications: Keeps staff aware of critical dates and alerts them to new assignments, rejected submissions, or system-wide announcements.

6. Ambassador Module

Roles: Ambassador

A specialized module for Strategy Ambassadors who monitor specific cross-departmental strategic initiatives and faculty-wide compliance.

Ambassador Dashboard

Provides a comprehensive overview of the specific strategic initiatives the ambassador is responsible for monitoring across their assigned faculty or cross-departmental area. Key UI elements include:

  • Faculty Hero Banner: A prominent blue gradient banner displaying high-level metrics: Faculty Strategic Progress, Area Activities, and Compliance Rate. It also breaks down total activities into On Track, In Progress, and Delayed.
  • Stat Cards: Four quick-glance cards showing Departments Oversaw, Area Risk Alerts, Compliance Average, and active Managed Units.
  • Departmental Progress Roster: A detailed table listing all sub-units or departments being monitored, complete with their individual activity count, visual progress bars, and status badges (e.g., On Track, Warning, Critical).
  • Faculty Risk Benching: A critical sidebar section highlighting specific risk alerts across departments, showing severity, progress, and upcoming due dates.
  • Faculty Submission Heatmap: A visual grid (heatmap) tracking the frequency and timeliness of submissions across different months for the entire faculty.

Other Ambassador Pages (Sub-sections)

  • Ambassador Reports: Specialized reporting tools used to generate insights on the adoption, compliance, and success of strategic initiatives across the specific faculties being monitored.
